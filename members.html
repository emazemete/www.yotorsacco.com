Membership Criteria & Benefits

Join Yotor Saving and Credit Cooperative Society Ltd and become part of a trusted financial community. Enjoy savings growth, affordable financial services, and long-term security.

Membership Criteria

Valid Government-Issued ID

Applicants must submit a valid government-issued identification document for verification.

Two Passport-Sized Photos

Two recent passport-sized photographs are required during the registration process.

Membership Contribution

Applicants must make the required membership contribution according to SACCO policy.

Completed Application Form

Every applicant must fill out and submit the official membership application form correctly.

Eligibility Verification

Applicants must meet the cooperative’s membership requirements and approval standards.

Minimum Savings Deposit

New members are expected to start with the required minimum savings deposit amount.

Benefits of Membership

Access to Loans

Members can apply for affordable and flexible loans to support personal and business needs.

Higher Dividends

Members enjoy dividend returns based on savings, shares, and cooperative performance.

Financial Security

Membership helps build stable savings habits and strengthens long-term financial security.

Safe Savings Opportunity

Your savings are managed in a trusted cooperative system designed for member growth and protection.

Community Support

Be part of a cooperative community that promotes unity, trust, and mutual financial support.

Business Growth Support

Members can access financial solutions that help grow small businesses and income opportunities.
