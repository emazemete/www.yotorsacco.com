Our History

Yotor SACCO was established with the vision of creating a strong financial cooperative that supports members through savings, affordable loans, and shared economic progress.

Since its foundation, the SACCO has worked to improve the financial well-being of its members by encouraging a culture of saving, responsible borrowing, and cooperative participation.

Over the years, Yotor SACCO has continued to grow in membership, strengthen its services, and build trust within the community through transparency, inclusion, and service excellence.

Today, Yotor SACCO remains committed to empowering individuals, families, and businesses through sustainable financial opportunities and cooperative values.
