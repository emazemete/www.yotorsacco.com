Our Services

Saving Services

Secure and flexible savings plans to help members build strong financial habits.

Our saving services encourage regular deposits, financial discipline, and long-term stability. Members can save for emergencies, education, family needs, and future investment opportunities.

Loan Services

Affordable and member-friendly loans for personal, business, and emergency needs.

Yotor SACCO provides accessible loan opportunities with fair terms, helping members improve livelihoods, grow businesses, solve urgent financial challenges, and invest in meaningful development.

Training & Consultant

Practical training and advisory support to improve financial knowledge and business growth.

We offer capacity-building programs, cooperative awareness, financial literacy sessions, and professional consultation to strengthen members, staff, and the wider community.

Member Support

Dedicated support and guidance for all members at every stage of their SACCO journey.

Our team helps members understand saving, loan access, account processes, cooperative participation, and service benefits to ensure a smooth and supportive experience.

Financial Growth Programs

Programs designed to promote financial inclusion, development, and long-term member success.

These programs help members improve budgeting, planning, income management, and sustainable financial growth while encouraging stronger community participation and resilience.

Business Support

Helping entrepreneurs and small businesses access opportunities through SACCO services.

Members with small businesses can benefit from financing support, financial advice, planning guidance, and cooperative opportunities that promote enterprise development.
